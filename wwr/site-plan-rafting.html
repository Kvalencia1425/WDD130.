Overview

Purpose

The purpose of our rafting website is to provide potential customers with comprehensive information about our rafting services, including trip options, safety measures, and pricing. The site aims to attract adventure seekers and outdoor enthusiasts, facilitate easy online booking, and provide a resource for preparation tips and customer testimonials. By establishing an online presence, we aim to expand our reach, improve customer engagement, and streamline the booking process.

Audience

Our target audience includes individuals and groups looking for thrilling outdoor activities, specifically those interested in white-water rafting adventures. This includes young adults, families, and corporate groups seeking team-building activities. The website is designed to appeal to both experienced rafters and beginners looking for a new adventure.

  • Scenario 1: A family of four is planning their summer vacation and wants to include an outdoor adventure that is both fun and safe for children. Answer: Our website provides detailed information on family-friendly rafting trips, safety protocols, and age-appropriate options.
  • Scenario 2: A group of friends wants to plan a weekend getaway and is looking for an exciting activity to do together. Answer: Our site offers a variety of trip packages that can be easily booked online, with options for different experience levels and group sizes.
  • Scenario 3: A company is looking for a unique team-building activity to boost employee morale and teamwork. Answer: Our corporate packages are tailored to provide an adventurous yet structured environment for team-building exercises, complete with facilitators and safety guides.

Color Palette

Our chosen color palette for the rafting site is designed to evoke the natural elements of water, earth, and adventure, creating an inviting and energetic atmosphere for our users. The colors selected are:

  • Primary Color: #396e94 (Steel Blue)
  • Secondary Color: #e7c24f (Gold)
  • Accent 1 Color: #a43312 (Burnt Orange)
  • Accent 2 Color: #381d2a (Dark Burgundy)

Typography

Headings Example Font

We have chosen 'Roboto' for our headings, which is a sans-serif font known for its clean and modern appearance, making it perfect for conveying important information clearly and effectively.

Normal Text/Paragraph Example Font

For the body text, we are using 'Open Sans', a highly readable sans-serif font that ensures easy readability across all devices and screen sizes.
